# 1. Biobank Needed to Build ML Models

## What is Biobank
A biobank is a centralized storage of:

- Blood samples
- DNA
- Medical images
- Patient health records
- Disease history

Example:

- UK Biobank → Used to train AI models for cancer prediction
- India can build "India Health Biobank"

## Why Biobank is Critical
AI needs real biological data to learn patterns such as:

- Cancer early detection
- Diabetes prediction
- Heart disease prediction

Without biobank → AI cannot become accurate.

## Strategic Opportunity for Your Startup
You can build:

- AI tools that integrate with biobank
- AI model training platform for hospitals

This is billion‑dollar infrastructure opportunity.
